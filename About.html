ABOUT THE GAME

TIC TAC TOE is a game of X's and O's. It basically includes two players where-in one player chooses X and other player chooses O. This game involves 3 X 3 grid where the player who choose X and will mark X in the any of the boxes of that grid then another player who chooses O will mark O in one of the boxes in that grid. The ultimate goal of this game is that if any of the two players completes 3 consecutive X or 3 consecutive O in any pattern in that grid will WIN!!! .
